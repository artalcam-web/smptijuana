SMP at our clinic starts at $699 USD. The average clinic in San Diego or Los Angeles charges $2,500 or more. Most clients save $1,500–$3,500 for the exact same technique and equipment.

How do I get there from San Diego?

Drive to San Ysidro and cross at the border — we're 5 minutes away at New City Medical Plaza. Park on the US side and walk across in ~10 minutes. Uber from the border is ~$3–5. Total trip from downtown San Diego: about 20 minutes.

How long does a session take?

2–4 hours per session. Most clients complete treatment in 2–3 sessions spaced 7–10 days apart — easy to schedule as a day trip.

Is scalp micropigmentation permanent?

Results last 3–5 years before a touch-up is needed. No surgery, no recovery time — you walk out the same day looking sharp.

How does SMP compare to a hair transplant?

SMP is non-surgical, costs 80–90% less than a transplant, and requires zero recovery time. It replicates the look of a freshly shaved head with micro-dots of pigment — ideal if you want immediate results without surgery.

What do I need to cross the border?

A valid US passport or passport card. No visa required for a short medical visit to Tijuana. Walking across the pedestrian crossing takes 5–10 minutes. Return to the US typically takes 10–20 minutes on foot.
